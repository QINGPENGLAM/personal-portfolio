    Pre Pare/Care

    Inspiration

    As the college application season has come to an end, I have seen that contemporary high school students are stressed out because they are unprepared.

    Purpose

    My app will create plans to help students find their intended major and activity. Over time, the user will know what he or she is interested in, and there will be enough projects to demonstrate interest. For users without direction, my app would recommend ways to discover their interests.
